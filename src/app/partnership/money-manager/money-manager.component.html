Money Manager

Introducing Brokers (IBs) are partners who manage and run one or several business portfolios under the coveted brand of Fullerton Markets.

Under the current volatile economic climate, Money Managers are in high demand. They play a critical role in managing client assets on our trading platform.

As a Money Manager in Fullerton Markets, you will receive the following perks:

  • Receive client assets in four different currencies
  • State-of-the-art Money Management Software
  • Manage unlimited accounts from one master account
  • Receive profession client reporting documents

Four methods of money managers

  • Lot Allocation
  • Percent Allocation
  • Proportional by Balance Allocation
  • Proportional by Equity Allocation
